Budget Planner

The Budget Planner is designed to be interactive and connect users together. I designed it so that it can be used for personal or business use. There are two types of users: admin and regular user. An admin can create a Budget Planner and have total control such as adding/deleting categories and items. Regular users can connect to the Budget Planner and only report the spending.

Tools: JavaFX, MySQL Workbench, SceneBuilder
