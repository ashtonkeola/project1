About Myself

I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a bachelor in Mathematics. This is my first year attending Illinois Institute of Technology in hopes of getting a master degree in Information Technology and Management. My concentration is Data Management. I was raised in Rockford, Illinois but I currently live in South Elgin.

Where I See Myself

My main goal in life is to become a data analyst for organizations that provide funding to help children go to school. During my undergratuate years, I was fortunate enough to become a social committee that provided funding for students whose families have low income. I learned that families income should not become a barrier to a students path to achievement. Data management is the backbone that keeps these organization alive because it keeps information of donors and donation trends. Therefore, my main goal in like is to provide creative and strategic advances to programs such as Illinois Promise.

Skills

My interest in information management began during my time interning for a real estate company. With my math degree, I was asked to explore data pertaining to house searches, customers, and successful sales. I spent long hours looking at patterns and finding new correlations. I learned SQL on my own. I found a liking to data because of the many advances and improvements it can make. I will plan on achieving the skills required for data management at the Illinois Institute of Technology.
